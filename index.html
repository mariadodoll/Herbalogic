Expanding your Herbal knowledge

Hello, welcome to my blog!! Very excited you have decided to stop by and visit. You’re probably wondering what is herbalism? Herbalism is the use of plants for medicinal purposes, and the study of botany for such use.

Here you will find a list of 5 plants some that you may have heard about and some that you may have not. These five plants are packed with powerful nutrients.

Water Lilly

The dried root and rhizome of the Water Lilly plant have been used to treat lung and gastrointestinal conditions. The leaves have been used externally to treat various skin conditions.

Strawberry

Strawberry leaves have been used externally for rashes, and internally for diarrhea, liver disease, kidney stones and as a diuretic.

Egyptian Onion/Garden onion

The bulb of the Egyptian Onion/Garden onion has many traditional uses including loss of appetite, fevers, colds, bronchitis, hypertension, inflammation and infection.

Dong Quai

Different parts of the root of Dong Quai plant are said to have different medicinal effects. The head of the root has anticoagulant activity, the main root has pain relieving activity and the tail of the root promotes blood circulation.

Common Milkweed

Common Milkweed is used in Native American medicine to treat a variety of disorders including asthma, bronchitis, kidney stones, ringworm, warts and moles. The sap of this plant has heart stimulating effects and should not be ingested.
